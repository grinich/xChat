# xChat Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 18, 2026_

xChat is a browser extension that restyles the X (Twitter) Direct Messages
interface into a full-screen, keyboard-first layout.

**xChat does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any user data.**

- xChat runs only on `x.com`.
- It reads the content of the X DMs page you are already viewing for the sole
  purpose of restyling it and providing keyboard shortcuts. All of this
  processing happens locally, in your browser, on your device.
- No data — including your messages, contacts, keystrokes, or browsing
  activity — is ever sent to the developer or any third party. xChat has no
  backend servers and makes no network requests of its own.
- xChat contains no analytics, telemetry, or tracking.
- xChat requests no storage permission and keeps no personal data.

Because xChat sends no data off your device, there is nothing to retain,
share, or sell.

Questions or concerns: please open an issue at
https://github.com/grinich/xChat/issues
